Thermal Insulation is a new must-have!
28/07/2015
Ivan Ivanov
Svetbytchimmash ltd GmbH

It doesn’t matter which part of the world you are living, you need an energy efficient and well insulated home to survive. If you are living in the North Eastern regions of the world like UAE or Saudi Arabia, then you need a proper insulation so that the intense heat and dust should not enter in your home. If you are living in the African regions, then you need proper insulation from heat and rain. Finally, if you are living in some cold regions of Europe like Canada or Switzerland, then you need a proper protection of the severe cold atmosphere. So you must need to make a well insulated and energy efficient home for your survival and well being. Thermal bridging may not affect the overall efficiency of your home, but you must control it to a certain level for maintaining the efficiency of your home. Thermal insulation and energy saving properties are a must for every home nowadays.

Thermal bridging is one of the weaknesses of your home that stops your home to become energy efficient. In thermal bridging, a more conducting material used in your home construction allows the heat to pass away easily through it, thereby creating an imbalance in heat transfer and insulation. One of the main reasons of thermal bridging is the use of different conductivity in your home insulation.

Thermal bridging becomes a major concern in many conditions when:

  • Due to a reasonable amount of heat outflow, there is a rise in heating cost and cost of energy consumption
  • Many regions will produce in your home, where the temperature may decline below the dew point. This leads to condensation, thereby producing moulds and other harmful bacteria within that region

Thermal bridging can be controlled by using proper insulation in faсades, doors and windows. Thermevo provides tools and a wide range of thermal insulation products to increase energy efficiency. For example, high-precision polyamide profiles for aluminium facades, doors, and windows. As you can see, in the first image, the outer side is connected to the inner side with a thermo-insulation profile in the second image. This is the most effective way for insulating aluminium fasades, doors, and windows.

Aluminum conducts heat and cold very well, therefore, in modern aluminum fasade, door and window systems the outer aluminum part is separated from the inner with help of a Thermal Insulation Profile. These profiles are made out of Polyamide, a material which conducts heat and cold approximately 1000 times less than Aluminium. Thermevo tools allow to optimize the thermal insulation system to lower energy consumption.
