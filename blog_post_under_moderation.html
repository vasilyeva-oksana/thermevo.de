When will the future arrive? Tomorrow or already today?
28/07/2015
Ivan Ivanov
Svetbytchimmash ltd GmbH
The Swiss Alps will see 380-meter skycraper, Tokio has 4-kilometer building. Is the future coming or is it just a fantasy of architects who work for PRs of corporations?

380-meter superhotel is desired to build on the edge of the alpine village Vals that is 1200 meters above the sea level. Its guests are expected to be rich alpine skiers from Asia and Middle East. About 100 rooms will be at their disposal. Cost of the cheapest room will be 1000 swiss francs (about 1000 dollars) per night, the most expensive one is 25 thousand. They have already made a financial plan. The highest building of the Old World will cost the ordering party, Remo Staffel, Vals ski resort owner, and the swiss businessman Paies Truffer, approx. 200 mil francs. The financial plan is, of course, preliminary and it is to be exceeded while building.
