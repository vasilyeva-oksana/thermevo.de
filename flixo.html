How to use?
To get the best result and spend the minimum time please read our tips
stage 1. Preparation
Please use only validated AUTOCAD PLUGINS
Please be sure your plugins are official and validated. Othervise we do not garantee correct processing of lines and objects.
Read more
Please remove all 3d objects
Please revove all limits (frames), fastener, screw, dimension lines, all the 3D elements and empty all cameras except foam.
Read more
stage 2. Processing
You can determine your own materials
When assigning materials, you can either use our database, and create your own. Please, be assured that nobody but you and Thermevo can see your settings.
Read more
Flixo autodetermines small areals and closes small gaps
Just to save your time we autodetermine material for metal constructions, thermoinsulation strips and seals. And also for areas smaller 2 mm2.

But it our assumption. You keep full control over the figure and can assign the material you need.
