It doesn’t matter which part of the world you are living, you need an energy efficient and well insulated home to survive. If you are living in the African regions, then you need proper insulation from heat and rain. Finally, if you are living in some cold regions of Europe like Canada or Switzerland, then you need a proper protection of the severe cold atmosphere. So you must need to make a well insulated and energy efficient home for your survival and well being.
